It keeps a live ledger of every point and mile you hold, across every card and programme. Three ways to feed it:

- **Talk to the AI editor** — say what you did (a purchase, a card payment, a points transfer, an award redemption) and it writes the correct entry, points and miles included.
- **Set a forwarding rule** — your bank's transaction emails land in MilesVault and get drafted hands-off.
- **Upload a statement** — to catch up on a whole month at once.

You review and approve; nothing's saved otherwise. Then the optimisation tools kick in:

- **Award Explorer** — route + cabin → every programme that flies it, the points cost, and whether you can afford it now or via a transfer.
- **Paths to Points** — every route from your cards to any programme: which points transfer, at what ratio — or what it'd cost to just buy the gap.
- **Status Match** — how to turn one airline/hotel status into another, free vs paid.
- **Where your money goes** — a drillable view of your spend and which card earns most where.

It's an invite-only beta, so expect rough edges (there's a feedback button — please use it). Your data is private; only you can see it.
